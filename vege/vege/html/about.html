About Us

UniversalFoodz exclusively caters to wholesale orders, with a minimum requirement of 100 packets per order. Each packet contains only 10 parathas and pooris, ensuring that our customers receive bulk quantities for their needs. This approach allows us to focus on providing high-quality products in larger quantities, making UniversalFoodz the go-to choice for those seeking wholesale quantities of our delicious frozen parathas and pooris. Our commitment to wholesale transactions enables us to meet the demands of businesses, events, or individuals with significant food requirements, while maintaining the excellence and convenience our products are known for.

Universal Foodz

UniversalFoodz takes pride in revolutionizing the frozen food market with its ready-to-eat parathas and pooris. Committed to delivering convenience without compromising on taste, the company employs top-notch freezing technology to preserve the authenticity of homemade delights. From classic aloo parathas to flaky lachha parathas, UniversalFoodz offers a diverse range of flavors, catering to various preferences. Embracing innovation, eco-friendly packaging, and a dedication to health, the company ensures its frozen delights provide not just a quick meal solution but also a wholesome and satisfying dining experience. With accessibility in mind, UniversalFoodz products are readily available in supermarkets, offering a convenient taste of home for individuals with busy lifestyles.
